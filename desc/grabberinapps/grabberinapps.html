Grabber in various apps

Description

Show Grabber in youtube, vlc and plex. Perfect with GrabberEX

This little tweaks enables the grabber that is often displayed in games in various apps with a player. To support more apps i created this tweak. Perfect for my other tweak [GrabberEX](https://repo.twickd.com/package/com.twickd.xqf.grabberex). GrabberEX shows the time and battery with a easy swipe gesture from top of your screen. Supported apps at this moment are: -Youtube -Plex -VLC Player If you want that i add more apps please contact me and i will see what i can do :) (First 2 pictures are plex, 3,4 are youtube)
